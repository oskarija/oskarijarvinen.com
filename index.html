About

I'm Oskari Järvinen, a designer based in Helsinki, Finland. With a background in music and photography, I bring a unique set of hands, eyes and thinking to any project.

My design skills range from print design to UX, UI and service design. One of my greatest strengths is my ability to work well with a variety of people and individuals. Whether it's collaborating with team members or working with clients, I understand the importance of effective communication and teamwork in achieving a shared goal.

I'm passionate about learning and believe that it is important to foster an environment that values personal growth. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to develop my skills. If you'd be interested in working together, feel free to reach out to me.
