b0nk - Drum Sound Generator

2023

UX Design

UI Design


We made a concept of a drum sound generator app designed to easily create and share sounds on the go.

Born out of the need for quick and easy access to drum sounds without the complexity of a full digital audio workstation (DAW), the concept of b0nk imagines a solution for on-the-go drum sound needs.

Why This Concept Was Made

The inspiration behind b0nk stemmed from a common frustration among musicians and producers who often find themselves in need of a quick drum sound. Opening a full DAW and different synths is a long process compared to visiting a website with any device.

Recognizing this, our team sought to develop a mobile-friendly solution that would allow users to effortlessly create, access and export drum sounds on their smartphones, catering to the need for simplicity and convenience in music production workflows.

Process

Our process began with research and ideation, where we identified the need for a user-friendly drum sound generator app. Through brainstorming sessions and interviews, we honed in on the key features.

Then we embarked on the design phase, focusing on crafting an intuitive user interface that would encourage creativity while minimizing complexity. Throughout the process, we conducted iterative testing and gathered feedback from musicians and producers to refine and enhance the app's functionality and user experience.

Outcome

Features include:

  • Intuitive UI design that simplifies sound creation and manipulation.
  • Knobs labeled only with graphics encourage experimentation and exploration.
  • Convenient preset saving and sharing functionality, allowing users to easily access and discover inspiring sounds.
  • Streamlined user experience that empowers musicians and producers experiment on-the-go.
